Variant 1 _NAP_h, _lat_corr and _lon_corr grids.

Of 144,781 floats (481 x 301) in the lat_corr and lon_corr columns of variant 1 only 55,139 resp. 55,348 are non-zero between indices [8598:93827] resp. lon_corr [8598:93830].

As a result, the 1st 28 and the last 169 rows (in total 197 rows, or 41% of 481) can all be represented by the same, single f-array of 301 zeros. There are other stretches of zeros, but none are 301 or longer.

The non-zeros in _lat_corr and _lon_corr tuples are initialled stored as 349 resp. 317 "runs of non-zeros" plus a start and end index. At import the runs are formatted into a row-order matrix (481 x 301).
