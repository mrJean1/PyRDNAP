Variant 1 _NAP_h, _lat_corr and _lon_corr grid columns.

Of 144,781 floats (481 x 301) in the _lat_corr and _lon_corr columns of original grid file rdcorr2018.txt only 55,139 respectively 55,348 are non-zero between indices [8598:93827] respectively [8598:93830].

As a result, the first 28 and the last 169 rows (in total 197 rows or 41% of 481) can all be represented by the same f-array of 301 zeros. There are other stretches of zeros, but none amount to 301 or more.

The non-zeros in _lat_corr and _lon_corr colums are initialled stored as 349 resp. 317 runs of non-zeros plus a start and end index. At import, the runs are formatted into a row-order matrix (481 x 301).
